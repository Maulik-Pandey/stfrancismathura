AboutST. FRANCIS SCHOOL NAVADA, MATHURA

St. Francis School, Mathura is an unaided Christian minority institution situated in Delhi-Agra National Highway with 6 acres of Land, established in 1992 by the Congregation of Missionary Brothers of St. Francis of Assisi (CMSF). It is a co-educational institution open to children of all communities. It follows the syllabus of the Indian School Certificate Examination Education ISCE, Delhi and the medium of instruction is English. At present there are 1462 students and 46 teachers, along with 14 non-teaching staff. With the recognition granted by the ISCE, the school has become a full-fledged institution with classes upto the secondary level.
We strive to inculcate in students a good philosophy of life based on faith in God, Respect for the individual, concern for the less privileged in society, and ideals of truth and justice.
